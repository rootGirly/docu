## Why this exists

I started documenting on Medium and GitHub Gists, but I needed a **self-contained, version-controlled** environment.

- **Control:** I own the data, the format, and the history. No platform dependencies.
- **Accessibility:** If I want to show a command snippet to my 14 years old cousin, he don't need a login. It's just Markdown.
- **Methodology:** I'm building my own standard operating procedure (SOP) for homelab security, automation, and everything I need.
- **Efficiency:** Instead of re-searching the same `iptables` rules or `docker-compose` configs, I write them here.
